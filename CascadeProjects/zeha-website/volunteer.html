Get Involved

Join our mission to transform lives and build stronger communities

Why Volunteer with ZEHA?

Make a Real Impact

Your time and skills directly contribute to improving lives and building sustainable communities across Zambesia.

Join a Community

Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for social change and community development.

Learn & Grow

Gain valuable experience, develop new skills, and broaden your perspective on global development challenges.

Cultural Exchange

Experience rich Zambesian culture while sharing your own knowledge and perspectives with local communities.

Volunteer Opportunities

Education Volunteers

Support our education programs by teaching, developing curriculum, training local teachers, or helping with school construction projects.

  • Teaching experience preferred
  • Curriculum development
  • Educational technology
  • Construction skills
Commitment: 3-12 months

Health & Medical Volunteers

Provide healthcare services, train community health workers, or support our health education and prevention programs.

  • Medical/nursing qualifications
  • Public health experience
  • Health education
  • Community outreach
Commitment: 6-24 months

Economic Development Volunteers

Help develop local businesses, provide microfinance training, or support agricultural and entrepreneurship programs.

  • Business development
  • Financial management
  • Agricultural knowledge
  • Marketing expertise
Commitment: 6-18 months

Environmental Volunteers

Support our climate action initiatives including reforestation, sustainable agriculture, and renewable energy projects.

  • Environmental science
  • Sustainable agriculture
  • Renewable energy
  • Conservation experience
Commitment: 3-12 months

Human Rights Advocates

Work on gender equality, human rights education, legal aid programs, and community empowerment initiatives.

  • Legal background
  • Human rights experience
  • Community organizing
  • Gender equality advocacy
Commitment: 6-18 months

Administrative & Technical Support

Support our operations with IT, communications, project management, fundraising, or administrative assistance.

  • IT and digital skills
  • Project management
  • Communications/marketing
  • Administrative experience
Commitment: 3-12 months

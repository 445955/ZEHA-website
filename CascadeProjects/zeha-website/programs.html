Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives creating lasting change across Zambesia

Transforming Communities Through Integrated Development

Our programs are designed to address the interconnected challenges facing communities in Zambesia. Through a holistic approach, we create sustainable solutions that empower individuals and strengthen entire communities.

Education Program

Building the foundation for a brighter future through quality education access and teacher development.

What We Do:

  • Build and renovate schools in underserved communities
  • Train and support local teachers with modern teaching methods
  • Provide educational materials and resources
  • Implement adult literacy programs
  • Offer scholarships for higher education
  • Develop vocational training programs

Impact:

25 Schools Built/Renovated
200+ Teachers Trained
3,000+ Students Enrolled

Health & Rehabilitation Program

Ensuring access to quality healthcare and rehabilitation services for all community members.

What We Do:

  • Establish and operate community health clinics
  • Train community health workers
  • Provide maternal and child health services
  • Implement vaccination and disease prevention programs
  • Offer rehabilitation services for disabled individuals
  • Conduct health education workshops

Impact:

12 Health Clinics
150+ Health Workers Trained
8,000+ Patients Treated

Poverty Alleviation Program

Breaking the cycle of poverty through sustainable livelihood opportunities and social support.

What We Do:

  • Provide microfinance and small business loans
  • Offer skills training and entrepreneurship programs
  • Support agricultural development initiatives
  • Implement food security programs
  • Provide emergency assistance to vulnerable families
  • Create community savings groups

Impact:

1,500+ Families Supported
800+ Microloans Provided
50+ Savings Groups
